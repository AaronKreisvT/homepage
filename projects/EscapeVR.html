Physics Lab Escape Room (VR)

A virtual-reality escape room inspired by the undergraduate physics laboratory.

Overview

  • Stack: VR
  • Status: Planing
  • Repo: GitHub

Notes

This project transforms the classical physics teaching lab into an interactive VR experience. Users must solve physics-based puzzles rooted in real experimental setups (mechanics, optics, electricity, thermodynamics) in order to “escape” the laboratory.
The environment is modeled after an actual university teaching lab, creating an authentic atmosphere for outreach events and prospective student days. Rather than abstract puzzles, challenges are embedded in realistic measurement procedures, instrument handling, and data interpretation tasks.
The aim is to combine scientific reasoning with immersive gameplay to make experimental physics accessible and engaging.

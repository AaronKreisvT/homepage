FPGA-VR

Connecting real-world sensor data with immersive virtual environments.

Overview

  • Stack: FPGA (Field Programmable Gate Array), Arduino, VR
  • Status: Planing
  • Repo: GitHub

Notes

This project explores the integration of physical hardware (e.g., Arduino-based sensor systems or programmable FPGA circuits) with a VR interface. Real-time measurements from temperature, light, magnetic, or electrical sensors are transmitted into a virtual laboratory, where data are visualized spatially and interactively.
Users can reconfigure circuits, modify parameters, and observe how real hardware responds through an immersive digital twin representation. The system investigates new forms of hybrid experimentation that combine physical instrumentation with virtual abstraction.
The objective is to explore novel interfaces for experimental physics education and digital laboratory concepts.
